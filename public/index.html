How to print, fold & cut
  1. Download the PDF and print on one side of a single sheet at 100% / actual size.
  2. Fold in half the long way (top to bottom), crease, open back up.
  3. Fold in half the short way, then in half again; open back up.
  4. Fold short way and cut the slit along the centre crease (middle two panels only).
  5. Open, fold long-ways again, push ends together so the cut opens into a +; collapse into a booklet.
