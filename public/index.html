How to print, fold & cut
  1. Download the PDF and print it on one side of a single sheet (Letter or A4), at 100% / actual size.
  2. Fold the sheet in half the long way (top edge to bottom edge), crease, and open back up.
  3. Fold in half the short way, then in half again to find the panels; open back up.
  4. Fold in half the short way and cut the slit along the centre crease (only the middle two panels).
  5. Open, fold long-ways again, and push the ends together so the cut opens into a +; collapse into a booklet and crease all folds.
